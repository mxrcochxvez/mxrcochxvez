## My name is Marco Chavez

Software Development has been a passion of mine for the past 7 years. Over those years of trying to learn, I’ve come to understand that making those mistakes turned me into a better developer. Some of my greatest accomplishments involve overcoming the fear of learning, asking for help, and communicating my mistakes to managers.

At Woz U I have helped over 30 students at a time, maintaining a pass rate of over 75%. I guide the students through study in rigorous topics like React, Springboot, C#, Java, JavaScript, HTML and CSS.

Whilst attending Woz U, I had to overcome the stresses of online learning which involved keeping motivated, and taking responsibility of moving forwards regardless of how many times I felt like I was losing direction. Some of the most important things I learned outside of the coursework during online learning was making sure to keep an office-like work-setting, keeping that setting organized, warning others that I was unavailable, and accepting the fact that there wouldn’t be too many people in my area that I would be able to study with.

I've had jobs in many different industries prior to attending Woz U. I worked as a fast food Worker, Manager, call center Agent, Salesperson, and Security Guard. Each experience I've had I learned from. Fast food taught me to work well in a team, Management taught me how to lead by example and the power of influence, Sales taught me how to communicate with others and ask questions, and Security Guarding taught me how to see past our immediate perceptions of people. I strive to be someone who is always learning and making the most out of my employment.
